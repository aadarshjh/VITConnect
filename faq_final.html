Frequently Asked Questions

What is VITConnect? +
VITConnect is a platform designed to help students manage their campus life at VIT. It provides access to mess menus, club registrations, events, campus maps, and more.
How can I register for clubs? +
You can register for clubs by navigating to the 'Club Registration' section. Fill out the form with your name, registration number, email, and select your preferred club.
How do I check the mess menu? +
Visit the Mess Menu page, select the day and mess type (Veg, Non-Veg, or Special) from the dropdowns to view the complete menu for that day.
Can I navigate the campus using VITConnect? +
Yes! Go to the Campus Directions page, select your start location and destination, and click 'Get Directions' to see the route on the map.
How can I give feedback? +
Visit our Feedback page built with React! You can rate different aspects of campus life and submit your comments.
